#### Summary
Existing KV cache pruning methods rely on input- or domain-specific thresholds for budget selection, causing significant degradation on open-domain inputs spanning diverse domains, lengths, and difficulty levels. ReFreeKV introduces a new "threshold-free" objective that adaptively adjusts KV cache budget allocation without requiring any pre-determined threshold, targeting full-cache-level performance retention. Extensive experiments across 13 datasets with diverse context lengths, task types, and model sizes demonstrate the method's efficacy and efficiency.
