#### Summary
Targets the problem that KV-cache quantization errors accumulate across timesteps during long-horizon autoregressive decoding, traced primarily to incorrect token scales. KVarN applies a Hadamard rotation to K and V followed by dual-scaling variance normalization along both axes, requiring no calibration data. At 2-bit precision it sets a new state-of-the-art on generative reasoning benchmarks including MATH500, AIME24 and HumanEval, with a vLLM implementation released.
