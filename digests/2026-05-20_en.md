#### Summary
Addresses accuracy loss when using MXFP4 to accelerate RL post-training of LLMs by proving an exact additive three-way decomposition of MXFP4 quantization error: scale bias from power-of-two rounding, deadzone truncation from zeroing small values, and grid noise from 4-bit nearest rounding.
