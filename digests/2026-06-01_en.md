#### Summary
OSCAR targets the accuracy collapse of INT2 KV cache quantization in long-context LLM serving by estimating attention-consumed covariance structures offline and deriving fixed rotations plus clipping thresholds aligned with downstream attention. It ships with a custom INT2 attention kernel compatible with paged KV cache and fused kernel pipelines, integrable into SGLang and vLLM. On Qwen3-4B-Thinking-2507 and Qwen3-8B the BF16 accuracy gap shrinks to 3.78 and 1.42 points (naive rotation INT2 collapses to near zero), and the method scales to Qwen3-32B and GLM-4.7 (358B) on par with BF16, staying robust on RULER-NIAH up to 128K. System-wise it cuts KV cache memory ~8x, raises throughput up to 7x at large batch sizes, and accelerates batch-size-1 decoding up to 3x over BF16.
