#### Summary
FP4 block-scaled attention on Blackwell GPUs suffers significant quality degradation in long-context settings. The authors observe that quantization error impact on the output is highly non-uniform and concentrates in a small number of query-key blocks containing the most important tokens. ThriftAttention uses a heuristic to select a small set of important query-key block pairs for FP16, computes the rest in FP4, and merges both paths via online softmax.
