#### Summary
LongLive-2.0 is an NVFP4-based parallel infrastructure spanning training and inference for long video generation. Training introduces Balanced SP, pairing clean-history and noisy-target temporal chunks per rank to give a natural teacher-forcing mask and SP-aware chunked VAE encoding, combined with NVFP4 to cut memory and speed up GEMM. Unlike Self-Forcing variants that need ODE initialization plus DMD, it directly tunes a diffusion model into a multi-shot interactive AR diffusion model, convertible to 2-step real-time generation via standalone LoRA. Inference on Blackwell uses W4A4 NVFP4 with NVFP4 KV cache and asynchronous streaming VAE decoding; non-Blackwell GPUs fall back to SP inference. Reported gains are 2.15x training and 1.84x inference speedup, with LongLive-2.0-5B hitting 45.7 FPS.
