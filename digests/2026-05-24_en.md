#### Summary
LongLive-2.0 is an end-to-end NVFP4-based parallel infrastructure for long video generation, targeting training and inference speed/memory bottlenecks. Training uses Balanced SP, which pairs clean-history and noisy-target temporal chunks per rank for a natural teacher-forcing mask with SP-aware chunked VAE encoding, and directly tunes a diffusion model into a multi-shot interactive AR diffusion model—skipping ODE init plus DMD—convertible to 4→2-step real-time generation via standalone LoRA. Inference on Blackwell GPUs runs W4A4 NVFP4 with NVFP4 KV cache and asynchronous streaming VAE decoding; non-Blackwell GPUs use SP inference to match speed. Achieves up to 2.15× training and 1.84× inference speedup, with LongLive-2.0-5B reaching 45.7 FPS.
