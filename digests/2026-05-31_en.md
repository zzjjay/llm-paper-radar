#### Summary
OSCAR tackles the accuracy collapse of INT2 KV-cache quantization for long-context LLM serving by estimating attention-aware covariance structures offline and deriving fixed rotations plus clipping thresholds aligned with what attention actually consumes. A custom INT2 attention kernel preserves compatibility with paged KV cache and fused pipelines, plugging into SGLang and vLLM. On Qwen3-4B-Thinking-2507 and Qwen3-8B, OSCAR shrinks the BF16 accuracy gap to 3.78 and 1.42 points while naive rotation INT2 collapses to near zero; it stays on par with BF16 on Qwen3-32B and GLM-4.7 (358B), and remains robust on RULER-NIAH up to 128K. System-side: ~8x KV-cache memory reduction, up to 7x throughput at large batch, and up to 3x batch-size-1 decoding speedup over BF16.
