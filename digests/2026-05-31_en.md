#### Summary
To make INT2 KV-cache quantization actually usable for long-context LLM serving, OSCAR estimates attention-aware covariance structures offline and derives fixed rotations and clipping thresholds aligned with what downstream attention consumes, instead of using attention-agnostic transforms like Hadamard. A custom INT2 attention kernel keeps compatibility with paged KV cache and fused pipelines, plugging directly into SGLang and vLLM. On Qwen3-4B/8B and scaling up to Qwen3-32B and GLM-4.7 (358B), OSCAR holds the BF16 gap to 3.78 and 1.42 points on the smaller models and stays effectively on par at larger scales, while naive rotation INT2 collapses; it also remains robust on RULER-NIAH up to 128K. System-wise it cuts KV-cache memory ~8x, raises throughput up to 7x at large batch, and accelerates batch-1 decoding up to 3x over BF16.
