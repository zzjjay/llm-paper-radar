#### Summary
Systematic evaluation of 8 LLMs under llama.cpp's K-quantization (2-bit Q2_K to 8-bit Q8_0) across MMLU-Pro (knowledge/reasoning), CRUXEval (code), and MuSR (reading comprehension). Higher precision consistently improves accuracy but with diminishing returns, while Q2_K usually retains acceptable accuracy though some models degrade substantially. Larger models are more resilient to aggressive quantization, and 7-9B mid-sized models offer the best efficiency-resource trade-off.

- 📊 Q8_0 best but with diminishing returns; Q2_K usually acceptable
- 📊 7-9B models hit the sweet spot for efficiency vs accuracy
- 🎯 Benchmarks 8 LLMs on MMLU-Pro, CRUXEval, and MuSR
- ⚠️ Some models suffer substantial accuracy drops at 2-bit
