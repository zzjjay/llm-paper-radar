#### Summary
Long-context LLM inference faces a dual bottleneck: existing KV cache reuse methods reduce prefill computation but ignore the storage and transfer cost of large KV caches, while naively combining KV compression with non-prefix reuse causes severe accuracy degradation. C²KV addresses this with a unified framework that learns a composable, compressed, position-agnostic KV cache manifold via a lightweight sidecar Extractor equipped with learnable compression tokens and structured attention flow. A compression-concatenation co-training strategy aligns extraction-time representations with their downstream reuse behavior without modifying the frozen base model. Evaluated across multiple long-context benchmarks and model families, C²KV achieves up to 17× inference speedup while preserving generation quality.

#### Summary
Training-free structured pruning methods rank attention heads and FFN channel groups independently, ignoring cross-module coupling through the shared residual stream, which can cause jointly indispensable units to be removed together. CoCurve performs a second-order Taylor expansion of the token-level KL divergence between a frozen model and its masked copy, yielding a Fisher matrix whose diagonal entries are classical node saliency scores and whose off-diagonal entries are co-pruning curvature terms capturing the extra damage from removing two units simultaneously. Under a single-ablation additivity approximation, the full M×M interaction matrix is recovered from M forward passes via a Gram product of ablation features, requiring no pairwise sweeps or gradients. Pruning is solved in one shot as a budgeted quadratic program over a shared attention–FFN budget, with no labels, fine-tuning, or recovery steps.

- 🎯 Second-order Taylor expansion of token-level KL yields a Fisher matrix with co-pruning curvature off-diagonal terms for joint attention–FFN pruning
- 💡 Full M×M interaction matrix recovered from M forward passes via Gram product under single-ablation additivity; no pairwise sweeps needed
- 🔧 Calibration-only, no labels or fine-tuning; one quadratic program solves the shared attention–FFN pruning budget in a single shot
- ⚠️ No benchmark results (PPL, MMLU, etc.) reported in the abstract; no comparison against standard baselines
